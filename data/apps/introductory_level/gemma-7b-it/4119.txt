```python
n, m = map(int, input().split())
x = list(map(int, input().split()))

x.sort()

# Calculate the minimum number of moves
moves = 0
current_position = x[0]
for i in range(1, m):
    if x[i] - current_position > 1:
        moves += abs(x[i] - current_position) - 1
    current_position = x[i]

print(moves)
```
