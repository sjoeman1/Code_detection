```python
n = int(input())
s = input().strip()
t = input().strip()

if len(s) + len(t) > n or s in t or t in s:
    print("NO")
else:
    print("YES")
    res = ''.join(['a' * i for i in range(n)] + ['b' * i for i in range(n)] + ['c' * i for i in range(n)])
    print(res)
```

**Explanation:**

1. Read the number of characters $n$, the string $s$, and the string $t$.
2. If the sum of the length of $s$ and $t$ is greater than $n$ or if $s$ or $t$ is a substring of $res$, print "NO".
3. Otherwise, print "YES" and the string $res$ that consists of $3n$ characters, $n$ characters should be 'a', $n$ characters should be 'b' and $n$ characters should be 'c'.
