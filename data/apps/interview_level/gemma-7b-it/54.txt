```python
w, m = map(int, input().split())

if w * m >= m:
    print("YES")
else:
    print("NO")
```

**Explanation:**

* Read the two integers `w` and `m`.
* Multiply `w` by `m` and check if the result is greater than or equal to `m`.
* If the result is greater than or equal to `m`, print "YES". Otherwise, print "NO".
